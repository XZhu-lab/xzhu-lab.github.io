Dr. ​Xiaoyue Tan (谭晓悦博士), Postdoc (2023-)


Email: xiaoyue.tan@connect.polyu.hk​
I am currently engaged as a postdoctoral fellow at Pride Lab, having completed my PhD at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My academic focus is on urban remote sensing, with a particular interest in pioneering applications of nighttime light remote sensing techniques and enhancing data quality. My educational journey includes a Master’s Degree in Geography from Beijing Normal University, obtained in 2019, and a Bachelor’s Degree in Geographical Information System from China University of Mining and Technology, achieved in 2016.

Alumni - Postdocs

Dr. Fei Xu (许飞博士), Postdoc (2022-2025)


Email: xufeibnu@gmail.com​
Fei Xu received his B.S. and M.S. degrees from Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology and Beijing Normal University. After completing his doctorate in Bioscience Engineering at KU Leuven in 2021, he worked as a Postdoc at Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He's currently an associate professor at East China Normal University. He is engaged in data processing research to make remote sensing data more suitable for monitoring the urban environment. In particular, he focuses on the data fusion technique to obtain synthetic image data with the advantageous properties (e.g., high spatial/spectral/temporal resolution) of different remote sensing data sources.
